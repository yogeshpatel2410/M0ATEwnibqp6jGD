ABOUT US

Kaizen, is a Japanese word which means "Kai" (change) and "Zen" (better). It is a Japanese philosophy based on the search for continuous improvement, based on the fact that small improvements can make us achieve big goals. Kaizen Care aims to provide our patients with advanced diagnostic and preventive care; complemented with compassionate medical treatment, all while adhering to the highest standards of medicine. The clinic offers specialized services delivered by skilled consultants who are highly experienced, compassionate and committed to providing quality health care.

We are a multi-specialty clinic providing a wide range of services related to Gastroenterology, Cosmetology and Dental care.  Our experienced doctors and team are committed in delivering a competent care for you and your family. We will seek excellence relentlessly to provide better service and deliver greater value to our patients. At Kaizen Care, we adopt 'STEEP'. 

  • ✓
    Safe: Avoiding harm to patients from the care that is intended to help them
  • ✓
    Timely: Reducing waits and sometimes harmful delays for both our patients and staffs
  • ✓
    Effective: Providing services based on scientific knowledge to all our patients
  • ✓
    Efficient: Avoiding waste, including waste of money and patient’s time
  • ✓
    Patient-centered: Providing care that is respectful of and responsive to individual patient preferences, needs, and values
