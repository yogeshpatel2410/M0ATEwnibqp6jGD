Gastroenterology Department @ KAIZEN CARE

Gastroenterology is the branch of medicine focused on the digestive systems and its various disorders.

"All is well when your digestion is well". At KAIZEN CARE, we make sure that we live by this saying. Good digestive health describes a digestive system that has appropriate nutrient absorption, intestinal motility, immune function and a balanced microbiota. 

The practice of gastroenterology concentrates on the diagnosis and medical management of diseases involving the esophagus, stomach, small intestine and large intestine (colon), liver, gallbladder, and pancreas and we provide prevention, diagnosis and treatment of all types of digestive conditions. Our best trained gastroenterologist provide a complete range of outpatient and endoscopy services to treat all aspects of gastroenterology, hepatology and pancreato-biliary diseases etc. Our gastroenterology department is complimented by our state-of-the-art endoscopy suite where various diagnostic and procedures are performed by our highly qualified gastroenterologist. 

Through an extensive evaluation, diagnosis and treatment of gastrointestinal and liver disorders, we provide patients with the most up-to-date medical care. We are proud to be a caring and empathic team of healthcare professionals who treat patients with compassion, dignity and respect. 

Our Services

Management of Gastrointestinal Condition

  • Gastrointestinal reflux diseases (GERD)
  • Eosinophilic Esophagitis
  • Peptic Ulcer and Gastric Ulcer
  • Helicobacter Pylori
  • Celiac Disease
  • Stomach discomfort (Gastritis, Non-ulcer Dyspepsia, Functional Dyspepsia, Indigestion)
  • Irritable Bowel Syndrome (Sensitive Gut, Constipation, Diarrhea)
  • Inflammatory Bowel Disease (Crohn’s Disease, Ulcerative Colitis)
  • Colonic Polyps
  • Diverticulosis
  • Barrett’s Esophagus

Diagnostics and Therapeutic Endoscopy 

  • Gastroscopy
  • Colonoscopy
  • Endoscopic Band Ligation and Sclerotherapy
  • Endoscopic GI Foreign Body Removal
  • Argon Plasma coagulation
  • Transnasal endoscopy
  • Nasojejunal and PEG Feeding Tube Placement
  • Intragastric balloon for obesity (BIB)
  • Surveillance (Barrett’s Esophagus, Gastric Ulcer, Ulcerative Colitis, Colon Cancer)

Liver Diseases

  • Alcohol Associated Liver Diseases
  • Liver Cirrhosis

Pancreatic Diseases
