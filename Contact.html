Your Gastroenterology Specialist in Bilaspur

Ready to take the next step towards better digestive health? We're here to help. Whether you need to schedule a consultation, ask a question, or find our clinic, all the information you need is right here. Our team is dedicated to providing you with prompt and compassionate assistance.

Booking Your Appointment: A Simple Guide

To ensure a smooth and efficient booking process, we've outlined the steps below.

Information to Have Ready

When you contact us by phone or WhatsApp, please have the following details ready. This will help us secure your appointment slot quickly.

  • Patient's Full Name
  • Age
  • Mobile Number

Additionally, please let our staff know if you are a returning patient or have a referral from another doctor.

Our Appointment Process

  • Reach Out: Your first contact via call or WhatsApp will be with our friendly administrative staff.
  • Book Your Slot: They will help you book an appointment with Dr. Sitendu Kumar Patel based on available slots and your preferences.
  • Confirmation: Once booked, you will receive a confirmation of your appointment time and date. All fees for consultations or procedures will be collected at the clinic during your visit.

A Note on Walk-In Visits

While booking a prior appointment is not mandatory, we highly recommend it. For walk-in patients, our staff will assist you with the next available slot, but this could result in a long waiting period. Booking in advance helps us organize our schedule and ensures you are seen in a timely manner.

Payment Information

For your convenience, we accept a variety of payment methods at our clinic.

  • Accepted Methods: UPI, Credit & Debit Cards, and Cash.
  • Insurance: Please note that we do not have direct tie-ups with insurance providers. We can provide a detailed receipt of your payment for any claims you wish to file independently with your insurer.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Do I need a referral to book an appointment?

While a referral is not mandatory, it can be helpful if another doctor has recommended a specialist consultation. You can book an appointment with or without a referral.

What are your consultation fees?

For information on consultation fees and any other charges, please contact our clinic directly by phone.

Do I need to fast or prepare in any way before my visit?

For an initial consultation, no special preparation or fasting is required. If Dr. Patel recommends a specific procedure that requires preparation (like fasting), our staff will provide you with detailed instructions at the time of booking that procedure.

What is the process if I need to cancel or reschedule my appointment?

We understand that plans can change. If you need to cancel or reschedule, please call us as soon as possible so that we can offer the slot to another patient.

I am a new patient. What can I expect during my first visit?

On your first visit, you will be greeted by our staff who will complete your registration. You will then have a detailed consultation with Dr. Sitendu Kumar Patel to discuss your health concerns. Afterwards, our staff will assist you with the payment process for the services you received.
