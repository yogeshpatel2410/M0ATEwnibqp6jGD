Meet ​Leading Gastroenterologist and Hepatologist

Dr. Sitendu Kumar Patel

Dr. Sitendu Kumar Patel, the founder and lead specialist at Kaizen Care, brings over 16 years of experience in diagnosing and treating complex medical conditions, digestive and liver conditions. A recognized expert in gastroenterology care in Bilaspur, Dr. Patel’s specialties include:

  • Digestive Tract Disorders: GERD, stomach ulcer treatment, IBS, and chronic bloating
  • Liver Conditions: Hepatitis, fatty liver, cirrhosis, and liver cancer
  • Pancreatic Disorders: Acute and chronic pancreatitis, pancreatic cancer
  • Advanced Endoscopic Procedures: Gastroscopy, colonoscopy, and ERCP

Dr. Patel is known for his compassionate approach and expertise, making him a trusted name for patients seeking a stomach and liver specialist in Bilaspur.
